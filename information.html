Social Media Cards

Snapchat is popular because it lets users share quick, fun moments with their friends through photos and videos. Many people love the filters, stories, and private messaging features that make communication feel more casual and personal. Users often turn to Snapchat to stay connected with close friends without the pressure of public posts. On average, Snapchat users spend around 30 to 50 minutes per day on the app, depending on their habits. It’s especially popular among younger audiences who enjoy the real-time, disappearing nature of the content.

Users go on Instagram mainly to share photos, watch videos, and stay connected with friends and trends. Many also use it to follow celebrities, brands, and influencers they admire. Some people use Instagram for shopping, entertainment, or finding inspiration for travel, fashion, or fitness. On average, users spend about 30 minutes to an hour each day scrolling, posting, and interacting. The app’s features like Stories, Reels, and DMs keep users engaged for longer periods.

People use Facebook to stay in touch with family, friends, and communities around the world. It’s popular for sharing life updates, photos, and participating in groups or events. Many also use it to discover news, watch videos, and follow pages that match their interests. On average, users spend about 30 to 40 minutes a day browsing their feed, commenting, and messaging. Facebook’s mix of social, entertainment, and information keeps people coming back daily.

People use TikTok to watch short, entertaining videos, discover trends, and create their own content. It’s a go-to app for everything from dance challenges to cooking tips and funny skits. Many users also enjoy following their favorite creators and exploring new topics through the "For You" page. On average, people spend about 60 to 90 minutes a day on TikTok, often losing track of time while scrolling. Its endless stream of personalized content keeps users highly engaged.
